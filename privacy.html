Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the AlgoPro mobile application ("the App") handles information.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All calculations, inputs, and results are processed locally on the user’s device.

Personal Data

The App does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information, including but not limited to name, email address, phone number, location data, or financial account details.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or external data collection services.

Data Storage

Any data entered into the App is stored locally on the user’s device and remains fully under the user’s control. The developer does not have access to this data.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the App Store.
